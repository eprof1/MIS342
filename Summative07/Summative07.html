Learning Objectives

Learning Objectives

  1. Design complex reports with appropriate data sources, sorting, and grouping.
    • Example: A manufacturing company designs a report to group production data by plant location and sort by output volume for operational analysis.
  2. Format reports to highlight duplicate values and display dates and page numbers.
    • Example: An educational institution formats reports to highlight duplicate student enrollments and include dates for academic records.
  3. Create professional titles and mailing labels within reports.
    • Example: A publisher generates mailing labels for book shipments, ensuring accuracy and professional presentation.
