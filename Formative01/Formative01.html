Learning Objectives

This exercise investigates collecting customer data to provide service to a customer.

After completing this assignment you will be able to:

  • determine when it is appropriate to use a spreadsheet to collect data
  • stipulate the limitations of spreadsheets in working with data
  • state the types of problems spreadsheets have in collecting data
  • state the benefits of using spreadsheets to collect data
